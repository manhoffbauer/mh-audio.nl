Set Anti Skating by ear

That is a method that is widely used.

You can play a number of test signals with the help of a test plate. A good test plate, for example, is the HIFI News Test LP.

On this record are a number of signals with increasing modulation, with which you can set the anti skating. Use headphones for this. However, the fact is that it is difficult for many people to make a good observation.

Then you could also measure with a scope. But you should just have that at your disposal.
