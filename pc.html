written by Peter Comeau

Peter Comeau has experienced virtually every aspect of the hi-fi business.
He started out in retail during the heyday of the Tottenham Court Road, then moved into magazine journalism.
He subsequently founded and ran Heybrook for many years prior to moving to China to oversee the design side of the IAG operation (which includes Quad, Mission and Wharfedale brands).
